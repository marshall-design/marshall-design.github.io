The Medium is the Massage

"Where a visual space is an organized continuum of a uniformed connected kind, the ear world is a world of simultaneous relationships."

"They suspect the ear; they don't trust it. In general we feel more secure when things are visible, when we can "see for ourselves."

"The phonetic alphabet forced the magic world of the ear to yield to the neutral world of the eye."

"Until writing was invented, man lived in acoustic space: boundless, directionless, horizonless, in the dark of the mind, in the world of emotion, by primordial intuition, by terror."

"The ear favors no particular "point of view.""
